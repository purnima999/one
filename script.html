ABOUT CONTEST

At FilmBanao, By entering our screenwriting competitions, you'll get your script read by executives, agents, and managers within the industry. If you win, you will get the reward money, and above that you may get a chance to be signed by an agent. Many of the writers who win these screenwriting contests end up signing a development deal or selling their winning script to one of the judges. Also, you can put "award-winning writer" at the top of your resume. While the competition is tough, screenwriting contests can be worthy to kick-starting a writing career. That's the whole reason you enter our screenwriting competition.


The Filmbanao Script Competitions has only one categories. 3 Minute Script Unlike other script competitions Filmbanao doesn’t just give awards or money as a prize but aims to get their winning scripts into production.
