ABOUT

The journey so far... “If everything was perfect, you would never learn and you would never grow.” Creation is an everyday process. Every day, people work in order to bring their content into existence, and every person is a creator. We, at FilmBanao, aim at discovering the creators, valuing and showcasing their creation for the world to see. We understand the difficulty of finding a platform that appreciates the process of creation, and the creation itself. Launched in January 2017, FilmBanao is one of the India’s finest innovative and youth-centric platform and is the premier source for promoting and exploring the talent that serves the need of dynamic film and TV industry in India and Abroad.

FilmBanao continues to meet and exceed its talent hunt mission by organising contest and talent hunt for all levels via online submission of short film making, script writing, storytelling and designing. FilmBanao spotlights both short script and short film competition intended to find extraordinary Talent among youths in India and abroad.


Candidates qualifying the Contest are rewarded with a prize money and been recommended to agencies, producers, and managers looking for talent in terms of emerging actors, directors, proof-of-concept or standalone shorts, and filmmakers across a broad spectrum of backgrounds. FilmBanao promotes individual actors and directors and plays an active role in getting content produced and distributed.
