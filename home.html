ABOUT

Flim is an art from that brings together people if all ages and backgrounds. it's also something with which children, young and old people can connect with and unlock their own creativity.

Flimbnao is platform for short film competition that, will be challenging filmmakers to create 2 minutes ultra-short masterpieces. Awards & Prizes

Films will be judged for their creativity and content as well as their technical ability. The winning filmmaker(s) each quarter will receive a goody bag, have their film showcased on the Into Film YouTube channel and be interviewed for the charity’s website.
If you win, you will get the reward money, and above that you may get a chance to be signed by an agent.
